Marmite on Toast

Love it or hate it here is the recipe for Marmite on toast.

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of white bread
  • Marmite
  • Butter

Method

  1. Toast the white bread until brown.
  2. Spread butter on one side of each slice of toasted bread.
  3. Spread a thin layer of Marmite on top of the butter.
  4. Enjoy!
