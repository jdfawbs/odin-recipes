Risotto

An authentic Italian style risotto dish, cooked the right way, slowly.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • 1 red onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 200g sliced mixed mushrooms
  • 500g Risotto rice
  • 125ml dry white wine
  • 1l of vegatable stock
  • 250g parmesan
  • 100g butter

Method

  1. Finely chop the onion and garlic.
  2. In the olive oil fry the onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add the sliced mushroom and cook of 2 minutes.
  4. Add the rice, stiring to absorbs all the fluid.
  5. Add the wine and stir until the alcohol has cooked off.
  6. Adding part of the stock to just cover the rice, stir adding adding stock gently ensuring the rice is just covered.
  7. Once all the stock is added turn off the heat and stir in the butter and parmesan.
  8. Leave to rest for 5 minutes and then serve.
