Risotto

An authentic Italian Lasagna, perfect comfort food.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • 1 Red Onion
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic
  • 500g Minced Beef
  • White Cheese Sauce
  • Chopped Tomatoes
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Fresh Pasta Sheets

Method

  1. Finely chop the onion and garlic.
  2. In the olive oil fry the onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add the minced beef and cook until browned off.
  4. Add the Italian seasoning.
  5. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook for 2 minutes.
  6. In a baking dish layer one layer of meat & tomatoes, then a layer of pasta sheets, then cheese sauce, the pasta, repeat these layers finishing with a layer of pasta topped with grated cheese.
  7. Bake in a 180c oven for 30 minutes
